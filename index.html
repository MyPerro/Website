At MyPerro

Our Mission

Our mission is to inculcate technology in the pet care industry to provide personalized care, foster transparency, and security. We aim to empower pet parents to cater to work commitments or plan vacations stress free.By also helping pet sitters and dog walkers get paid according to their efforts thus forming a chain of trust, care , love and contentment on both sides

Our Vision

Our vision is to broaden our services to ensure your dog gets the utmost personalized care that it deserves.We aim to have our own boarding area- “MyPerro Space” and also expand into pet grooming,pet-friendly commute and pet food industry.We want to provide the best personalized care by understanding the unique needs of every dog coming to us.

Our Services

Doggy Daycare

Your dog spends the day at your sitter’s home. Drop them off in the morning and pick up a happy pup in the evening.

Smart Dog Collars

These collars monitor your dog's real-time location and activity, allowing us to keep a more accurate check on their well-being.

24/7 Video Monitoring

Stay connected with your pet even when you're apart. Our 24/7 video monitoring lets you check in on your dog anytime, giving you peace of mind.

Dog Sitting

Our sitter takes care of your pets either at their or your home. Your pets will get all the attention and personalized care they need when you are on a vacation.

MyPerro Boarding

Your pets stay overnight in your sitter's home. They'll be treated like part of the family in a comfortable environment. Our Open play areas provide dogs enough room to socialize and exercise.

Certification Programs

Our flagship partner certification program allows individuals or families to get verified for boarding pets.
